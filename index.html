Hongbo Jiang is currently a Full Professor (with University Professorship, 岳麓学者) in the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engeering, Hunan University. Previously he was with Huazhong University of Science and Technology as a professor. He received Ph.D. in Computer Science from Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Case Western Reserve University. Now his research focuses on computer networking, especially, IoT, and mobile computing and sensing. Here are some ongoing projects:

  • Mobile and wireless applications.
  • Privacy in mobile systems.
  • Geometric algorithms for wireless and sensor networks.
  • Energy-efficient data processing in wireless and sensor networks.

Opening Positions: (1) I am currently recruiting self-motivated and dedicated Ph.D./M.S. students with solid background in mathematics, wireless communications and networking, etc. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested. (2) I plan to host a selected number of PostDocs with strong research background.
